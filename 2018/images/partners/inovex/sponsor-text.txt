inovex is an IT project house with a focus on digital transformation.
More than 250 IT experts help companies in digitising their core competencies and implementing new value-added models. inovex's portfolio includes data products, web and app development, smart devices, IoT and robotics, replatforming, microservices and devops, big data, data science and deep learning, search, data centre automation, cloud infrastructures and hosting, trainings and coaching. inovex has offices in Karlsruhe, Pforzheim, Stuttgart, Munich, Cologne and Hamburg and is involved in projects across Germany.
